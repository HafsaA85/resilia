# Resilia 🌿

Resilia is a gentle web platform designed to support women in understanding anxiety, building emotional clarity, and developing quiet confidence — one step at a time.

It offers a safe and private space for reflection, journaling, and personalised therapeutic support without pressure or perfection.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 Anxiety Triggers Tracker
Log situations, thoughts, feelings, and intensity levels to identify emotional patterns and understand anxiety responses through a CBT-informed lens.

### ✍️ Personal Journal
Write private reflections linked to specific triggers or as free journaling entries in a calm, distraction-free space.

### 🎯 Personalised CBT Exercises
Resilia recommends supportive exercises based on the user’s anxiety intensity, such as grounding, reframing, breathing, and self-soothing techniques.

### 🔥 Reflection Streak
Visual streak showing recent journaling consistency to gently encourage self-awareness habits.

### 🌱 Insights Dashboard
View average anxiety levels, recent activity, and common triggers to build emotional clarity over time.

### 🌙 Calm & Faith-Friendly Design
Created to feel reassuring, gentle, and emotionally safe, respecting sensitivity and inner reflection.

### 🏢 Organisation Wellbeing Access
Supports workplace wellbeing programmes through organisational licences while keeping all personal reflections fully private.

### 🔒 Private & Secure
All reflections, triggers, and journals are user-specific and protected by authentication.  
Organisations can never access individual mental health data.

---

## 🧠 Personalised Support Approach

Resilia adapts support based on anxiety intensity:

| Intensity | Support |
|----------|--------|
1–3 | Gentle reflection |
4–6 | Cognitive reframing |
7–8 | Grounding & regulation |
9–10 | Stabilisation & soothing |

This allows the platform to offer the right level of therapeutic guidance at the right moment.
